Basgiath War College

Jump into the wonderous world of Fourth Wing. Join the Riders Quadrant or the Scribes! You can go through training and Threshing and possibly bond with one of the dragons. Who knows, maybe you'll find yourself a Xaden Riorson. Or you get incinerated by one of the dragons. There are so many options for your story in this beautiful land of dragons, destruction, betrayal and war.

Night Court

Find yourself in the beauty of Prythian, the land of the Fae. Choose which Court you wish to live in and find yourself among some of the awesome creatures! You can go hunting for the Suriel or desperatly try to escape the Bogge and Naga. If you are lucky you may come across your mate. If you are unlucky it might be the High Lord of Spring. Let him have some happiness, he really needs some.

Hogwarts

Go back to the classics and relive your Harry Potter phase! We offer options to go to either the Golden Trio Era or Marauders Era! Get sorted into your Hogwarts house, make friends and enemies, perhaps get killed by a Death Eater if that tickles your fancy. For some extra charge you can even choose which family you want to be born in! Especially designed for the people who want to experience the traditions of the Noble Black family line.
